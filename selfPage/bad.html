Who Am I

A blossoming youth with a burning fire to begin making a difference in the world. For 5 years I have been in a cocoon, cultivating my ability to create via computation. I have been in a state of observation, learning about myself and the world through a process of rigorous discipline. I have been learning how to learn and how to think critically. However, I can no longer suppress the desire to create, to produce, to see my thoughts manifest into reality.

A Little History

A Student in his last semester of a computing science degree at University of Alberta, Edmonton Canada. I grew up in Yellowknife, NT, which is far north in Canada. Thus far in my life I have had several passions; basketball, math, physical training, competitive gaming - Warcraft 3 Starcraft 2 and Super Smash Bros. Melee - cooking, yoga, school work, and the exploration of thought.
